  2. Manager position requirements

    Career Definition
    Real estate manager will oversee the operational aspects of commercial and residential properties and is responsible for maintaining the premises and increasing their value. you will provide services for multiple buildings or locations. Job duties associated with large properties can include training and supervising staff members and groundskeepers.also responsible for daily services and minor repairs. additional activities can include making mortgage, tax and insurance payments, as well as other bookkeeping activities.
    Education Required
    applicants should have a bachelor's degree in real estate, finance or business administration. Managers often receive on-the-job training, including how to operate and repair any machines or equipment located on the premises.
    Skills Required
    must have excellent interpersonal and analytical skills, including the ability to communicate with owners, staff and tenants in an effective and efficient manner. Computer literacy, record keeping and strong leadership abilities are also required. Manual dexterity and knowledge of maintenance tools and equipment may be helpful in more hands-on positions.

  3. Agent jobs

    Career Definition
    Rent, buy, or sell property for clients. Perform duties, such as study property listings, interview prospective clients, accompany clients to property site, discuss conditions of sale, and draw up real estate contracts. Includes agents who represent buyer.
    Responsibilities
    1. Provide guidance and assist sellers and buyers in marketing and purchasing property for the right price under the best terms
    2. Determine clients’ needs and financials abilities to propose solutions that suit them
    3. Intermediate negotiation processes, consult clients on market conditions, prices, mortgages, legal requirements and related matters, ensuring a fair and honest dealing
    4. Perform comparative market analysis to estimate properties’ value
    5. Display and market real property to possible buyers
    6. Prepare necessary paperwork (contracts, leases, deeds, closing statements etc)
    7. Manage property auctions or exchanges
    8. Maintain and update listings of available properties
    9. Cooperate with appraisers, escrow companies, lenders and home inspectors
    10. Develop networks and cooperate with attorneys, mortgage lenders and contractors
    11. Promote sales through advertisements, open houses and listing services
    12. Remain knowledgeable about real estate markets and best practices
    Requirements
    1. Proven working experience as a real estate agent or real estate salesperson
    2. Proven track of successful sales record
    3. Ability to work independently combined with excellent interpersonal skills
    4. Strong sales, negotiation and communication skills
    5. Pleasant and trustworthy
    6. MS Office familiarity
    7. Real estate agent’s or broker’s licence
